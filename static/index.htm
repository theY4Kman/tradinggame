Trading Game

Instructions

Goal: You've got five minutes to make as much money as you can.

Make money by purchasing items. The price of each item is shown, along with the market value. When you buy an item, it will automatically be put up for sale at its market value. Your profit for each item is the difference between its price and market value.

Be careful, because not all sellers are trustworthy. A seller might not ship your item after you buy it. The Seller Ratings give you an indication of the seller's past history.
